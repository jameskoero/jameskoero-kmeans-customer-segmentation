# Changelog

All notable changes to this project are documented here.

## v1.0.0 - June 2026

### Added
- K-Means clustering on Mall Customers dataset (K=5)
- Elbow Method for optimal K selection
- StandardScaler preprocessing pipeline
- 6 visualisations: elbow curve, scatter plot, radar profiles,
  business insights bar chart, segment distribution pie chart,
  revenue gap analysis chart
- Business strategy per cluster with Kenyan market localisation
- M-Pesa and Safaricom channel recommendations per segment
- Full reproducibility: notebook, CSV, requirements.txt
- MIT License

### Environment
- Built entirely on Android using Google Colab
- No laptop, no GPU, no paid tools
